3564 low budget living units completed under Naya Pakistan Housing Program

Some 3,564 low-cost housing units from the Workers' Welfare Fund (WWF) and 18,499 from the Akhuwat Foundation have been completed under the Naya Pakistan Housing Scheme (NPHP), according to information provided to the Senate by the Minister of State for Law and Justice Shahada Awan (NPHP). The Minister informed that housing finance applications from 9,563 candidates selected in the ballot for housing under construction are being processed. The Federal Employee Housing Authority (FGEHA), the Workers' Welfare Fund and development authorities work together to deliver low-cost housing projects.
